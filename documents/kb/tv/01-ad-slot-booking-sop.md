# Ad Slot Booking SOP — MCN TV

## Purpose

This document describes how the Sales & Traffic team books, confirms, and
manages advertising slots across MCN TV's national broadcast schedule.

## Rate Card Tiers by Daypart

Ad slots are priced by daypart, reflecting each slot's typical audience
reach:

- **Prime Time (19:00–22:00)**: highest rate tier. Requires the longest
  lead time to book (minimum 14 days before air date) due to high demand.
- **Day Time (06:00–17:00)**: mid rate tier. Minimum 7 days lead time.
- **Late Night (22:00–01:00)**: lowest rate tier. Minimum 3 days lead
  time, and slots can often be booked on short notice.

Rates within each tier are further adjusted by the historical average
rating of the program the slot is attached to — a slot on a program with
a rating above 10% carries a premium multiplier of 1.3x over the base
daypart rate.

## Booking Process

1. The advertiser (directly or via an agency) submits a slot request to
   Sales & Traffic, specifying the target program(s), daypart, and
   campaign flight dates.
2. Traffic checks slot availability against the current schedule and
   responds with a confirmed slot or alternatives within 2 business days.
3. Once the advertiser confirms, Traffic issues a booking confirmation
   and locks the slot in the schedule. Payment terms follow standard
   Finance invoicing (net 30 for existing advertisers, prepayment for
   new advertisers).
4. Creative materials (the ad itself) must be submitted at least 5 days
   before the first scheduled air date for compliance review (see the
   Content Standards & Compliance SOP).

## Cancellation and Make-Good Policy

If a booked program underperforms its guaranteed rating (the rating
the rate card was based on) by more than 15%, the advertiser is entitled
to a **make-good**: an additional slot of equivalent value at no extra
charge, scheduled within 30 days of the original air date.

Advertiser-initiated cancellations:

- More than 7 days before air date: full refund, no penalty.
- 3–7 days before air date: 50% cancellation fee.
- Less than 3 days before air date: no refund; the slot may be resold by
  Traffic at its discretion.
