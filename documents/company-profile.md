# MCN Group — Company Profile

## About MCN Group

MCN Group is a media and entertainment conglomerate headquartered in
Jakarta, Indonesia. Founded to bring together broadcasting, digital
streaming, and news under one group, MCN Group reaches audiences across
free-to-air television, on-demand streaming, short-form video, and digital
news.

MCN Group operates three business units, each serving a distinct audience
and revenue model, while sharing common back-office functions (HR, Finance,
IT, Legal) at the group level. One of these units, MCN+, spans two
products (OTT streaming and short-form micro-drama) under a single P&L and
leadership team.

## Vision

To be Indonesia's most trusted media and entertainment group, delivering
stories that inform, entertain, and connect audiences across every screen.

## Mission

- Produce and distribute high-quality entertainment and news content across
  broadcast and digital platforms.
- Build direct, sustainable relationships with audiences through streaming
  and short-form video.
- Uphold journalistic integrity and editorial standards across all news
  properties.
- Operate efficiently as one group while letting each business unit move at
  the speed its market demands.

## Business Units

### 1. MCN TV — Free-to-Air Broadcasting

MCN TV operates national free-to-air television channels, producing and
airing entertainment programs (drama series, variety shows, reality TV) and
licensed content. Revenue is primarily **advertising-based**, driven by
program ratings and audience share.

- **Audience**: General national audience, all age groups.
- **Revenue model**: Advertising slots sold against program ratings.
- **Key metric**: Viewership rating / audience share per program, per time
  slot.

### 2. MCN+ — Streaming & Short-Form Video

MCN+ is MCN Group's digital video business unit, run as one P&L under one
director, spanning two distinct products that share subscriber and
content-licensing operations: a subscription streaming platform and a
short-form micro-drama app.

**Product: MCN+ (OTT Streaming)** — a subscription video-on-demand (SVOD)
platform offering a content library of MCN-produced series, licensed
movies, and exclusive originals, alongside simulcast of select MCN TV
programs.

- **Audience**: Digitally-native viewers seeking on-demand, ad-light
  content.
- **Revenue model**: Subscription tiers (monthly/annual), with a limited
  ad-supported free tier.
- **Key metric**: Active subscribers, watch time, content completion rate,
  churn rate.

**Product: MCN+ Shorts (Micro-Drama)** — a vertical, mobile-first
short-form drama product distributed through its own app and social
channels. Content is produced in short episodes (1–3 minutes) optimized
for mobile viewing, following the micro-drama format popularized across
Asian markets.

- **Audience**: Mobile-first, younger demographic, high-frequency short
  session viewers.
- **Revenue model**: Freemium — early episodes free, later episodes unlocked
  via in-app coin purchases (micro-transactions); limited ad placements
  between episodes.
- **Key metric**: Coin purchase revenue, episode unlock rate, completion
  rate per title.

### 3. MCN News — News Media Division

MCN News operates MCN Group's news properties across TV news broadcasts,
a news website, and a news mobile app, covering national, business, and
regional news.

- **Audience**: General public seeking news and current affairs, plus a
  business/professional segment.
- **Revenue model**: Digital advertising, sponsored content, and traffic
  from the news website/app; TV news slots also carry advertising.
- **Key metric**: Article page views, audience engagement, unique visitors.

## Organizational Structure (High Level)

```
CEO, MCN Group
├── Director, MCN TV
├── Director, MCN+
│   ├── Head of Streaming (OTT)
│   └── Head of Shorts (Micro-Drama)
├── Director, MCN News
└── Group Functions (shared across all units)
    ├── Group HR
    ├── Group Finance
    ├── Group IT
    └── Group Legal & Compliance
```

Each business unit has its own operational team (content production, sales,
programming/editorial) and relies on Group Functions for shared services.

## Market Position

MCN Group competes in a media landscape where:

- **FTA TV** competes for advertiser budgets against other national
  broadcasters, with success driven by program ratings.
- **MCN+** competes on two fronts under one P&L: its streaming product
  against local and global SVOD platforms for subscriber attention and
  retention, and its Shorts product in the fast-growing micro-drama app
  category, where user acquisition and monetization speed matter more than
  catalog depth.
- **MCN News** competes with other digital news outlets for traffic and
  reader trust, alongside broadcast news competitors.

MCN Group's advantage is cross-promotion: content and audiences can move
between FTA, streaming, and short-form properties, and news coverage can
amplify entertainment content (and vice versa).
