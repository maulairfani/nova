# Shorts Coin Purchase and Refund Policy — MCN+

## Purpose

This document defines how MCN+ Shorts' coin-based unlock system works and
the refund rules Subscriber Support applies for coin-related disputes.

## Coin Packages and Unlock Pricing

- Coins are sold in fixed packages (100 / 500 / 1,200 / 3,000 coins),
  with larger packages carrying a lower effective per-coin price as a
  volume incentive.
- The first 3–5 episodes of every Shorts title are free to unlock; later
  episodes cost a per-episode coin price set by the Content Licensing
  team at title onboarding (see the Content Licensing and Catalog SOP),
  typically 10–30 coins per episode depending on production tier.
- An unlocked episode remains permanently accessible to that subscriber's
  account — coins are a one-time unlock cost, not a rental.

## Purchase Process

1. Subscriber selects a coin package in-app; payment is processed via the
   platform's in-app billing (App Store / Play Store) or direct payment
   methods where available.
2. Coins are credited to the account balance immediately on payment
   confirmation. Failed payments credit no coins and are logged for
   reconciliation.
3. Episode unlocks are deducted from the coin balance at the moment of
   unlock and are non-reversible from the subscriber's side (no
   self-service "re-lock" to recover coins).

## Refund Policy

- **Accidental unlock, reported within 24 hours**: full coin refund to
  the account balance (not a cash refund), one exception per subscriber
  per calendar month.
- **Payment processed but coins not credited (technical failure)**: full
  coin credit once Subscriber Support verifies the payment record,
  target resolution within 2 business days.
- **Dissatisfaction with content after unlocking**: not eligible for a
  coin refund — this is disclosed at time of purchase and is consistent
  with standard digital-content-sale practice.
- Any refund request beyond these categories is escalated to the
  Subscriber Support Lead for a case-by-case decision, logged with a
  resolution and rationale for monthly policy review.
